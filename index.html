ABOUT ME

Having lived my whole life in the South of France, I've had the privilege of growing up surrounded by multiple cultures.

At home, I spoke both German and English, while attending French schools an environment that shaped my curiosity for communication and creative expression.

Today, I am pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Communication Sciences at the University of Côte d'Azur in Nice, with a special interest in media, storytelling, and digital branding.

From an early age, I dedicated over seven years to intensive dance training in ballet, jazz, and contemporary dance, even completing an internship with Les Ballets de Monte-Carlo. Dance has always been a core part of who I am a language of movement and emotion that naturally evolved into my passion for film, photography, and visual storytelling.

At 17, I began exploring new creative paths, working on short films, digital media projects, and various fashion and commercial campaigns.

Since 2020, I've been combining my studies with my work as a model and actress, collaborating with brands such as McDonald's, Monte Carlo Beer, and Ema Fragrance, as well as on productions for Disney, Nikon Film Festival, ZDF, and KIKA.

I love embodying different personalities, finding inspiration in every creative challenge, and continuously learning through experience. My background in dance and communication helps me approach each project whether on camera or behind it with a sense of precision, emotion, and authenticity.
